Esprima ECMAScript parsing infrastructure for multipurpose analysis

Esprima (esprima.org) is an experimental ECMAScript (also popularly known as JavaScript) parsing infrastructure for multipurpose analysis. It is also written in ECMAScript.

Esprima is still in the development, for now please check the wiki documentation.

Since it is not comprehensive nor complete, refer to the issue tracker for known problems and future plans.

Feedback and contribution are welcomed! Please read the contribution guide for further info.
